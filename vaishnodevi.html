About Maa Vaishno Devi

The Maa Vaishno Devi Temple, nestled in the Trikuta Mountains of Jammu & Kashmir, is a sacred pilgrimage site revered by millions. The temple represents the divine power of Maa Durga and symbolizes strength, purity, and prosperity.
